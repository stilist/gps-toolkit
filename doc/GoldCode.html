maximum_sequence_length :number

LFSRs have a maximum of 2**m states. One of those is fairly useless: if seeded with all 0s, the LFSR will never transition to any other state. However, LFSR can never transition into that state either, so the LFSR can cycle through a maximum of 2**m - 1 states.
